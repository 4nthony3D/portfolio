About

Anthony Molina

I'm Anthony Molina, a 3D artist specialized in stylized characters for film, advertising and animation. I'm 21 years old and based in Guayaquil, Ecuador. I'm passionate about digital art and have experience across the full 3D production pipeline, from digital sculpting and modeling to look development and final rendering.

My main tools are ZBrush, Maya, Blender, Marvelous Designer, Substance 3D Painter, Arnold, XGen, Advanced Skeleton and Studio Library. I also have complementary experience in rigging, basic animation, lighting, rendering and look development (lookdev), as well as in the Adobe suite for editing, compositing and project presentation.

I'm looking to join a studio where I can grow as an artist and contribute to productions with memorable characters. I'm disciplined, self-taught, and always striving to push my own technical and creative limits.
